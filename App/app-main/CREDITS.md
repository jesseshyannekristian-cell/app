## ASCII Art (fan-made, original)
All ASCII art shown in the game (`game/art.py`), including the **SCP-173** depiction, is **original,
fan-made work created specifically for this project** and is released under **CC BY-SA 3.0**.

> **Important:** None of the ASCII art is traced from, generated from, or otherwise derived from any
> photograph or copyrighted depiction of any SCP. In particular, the SCP-173 art is a deliberately
> abstract, stylised original and is **NOT** based on the "Untitled 2004" sculpture (by Izumi Kato)
> historically associated with SCP-173 — that image was removed from the SCP Wiki over its licensing,
> and this project intentionally avoids it. Only the freely-licensed SCP-173 *textual concept* (under
> CC BY-SA 3.0) is referenced.
